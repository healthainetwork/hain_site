Our Mission

Health AI Network is an open multidisciplinary community of students and professionals interested in using AI effectively to address needs in healthcare.

Our Mission is to advance proper understanding, development, and adoption of AI in healthcare.
We believe that artificial intelligence technologies can make health care more accessible, affordable, accurate, & humane.
We also believe it is critical to enable health care professionals to participate in the ideation, development, and implementation of health AI solutions.

We enable relevant stakeholders across industries to learn of each others' needs & capabilities with the hope of impactful collaboration via providing platform & resources for:

Learning: We discover, curate, and develop content to equip stakeholders in clinical service, business, and data science with the essential language and perspective to leverage AI effectively. Our current offerings are annual Seminar Series and Health AI Newsletter. Stay tuned for our practical Primer (to be released this winter)!

Action: We identify low-hanging problems in healthcare ripe for AI application and empower motivated individuals to tackle. Our Team is actively setting up the following to make available to our wider Community in January 2019: - Clinical Data Challenge: crowdsourcing best data science solution for a well-identified problem with well-prepped data - Incubator: providing a structured program with necessary resources & supportive environment to help entrepreneurially-wired members to take the first crucial steps in building a commercial health information technology solution.
